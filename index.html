// About Electra

Engineered, not improvised

Electra is an electrical engineering consultancy based in Lebanon, providing design, installation and maintenance services for residential, commercial and industrial clients.

With the surge in solar installations across Lebanon, many systems are being installed without proper engineering oversight — leading to undersized systems, safety issues and equipment that fails early. Electra's approach starts with engineering: every system is sized, designed and documented by a qualified electrical engineer before any equipment is installed.

This means longer-lasting systems, accurate sizing for your actual needs, and documentation you can rely on if you ever need warranty support, expansion, or resale of your property.
